About Us

The Global Athlete Alliance focuses on providing services and supports to the community and all of althletes, by supporting athletes and trainers across the globe. By uniting memory sports organizations around the globe, we aim to promote the benefit of sports to everyone in the world.

About Herb Jones

Herb Jones is the coach for hurdles, long sprinting and endurance (mid-distance / distance) student-athletes. He provided training guidance that helped lead Zachary Charles to be crowned as the 300-meter intermediate hurdles 6A 2019 Georgia High School Association State Championship gold medalist and Nahil Perkins to become the runner-up silver medalist in the same race. Coach Jones is an IAAF Level 5 certified youth specialist, USATF Level 3 certified youth specialist, USATF Level 2 certified Endurance specialist, USATF Level 2 Certified Sprints, Hurdles & Relays specialist, and USATF Level 1 Certified Specialist. Additionally, he is certified in the following:

  • USTFCCCA Certified Sprints, Hurdles, & Relays Coaching
  • USTFCCCA Certified in Track & Field Technical Coaching
  • Georgia High School Association (GHSA) Certified Community Coaching
